# nopCommerce AI Chatbot

The **nopCommerce AI Chatbot** plugin adds a smart, floating chat widget to your nopCommerce storefront. It uses artificial intelligence to answer customer questions in real time, reducing the need for manual support.

Once configured correctly, the chatbot will:

- Appear as a **floating button** on your store pages
- Answer customer queries using **AI powered by OpenRouter**
- **Remember recent conversation context** for natural, follow-up conversations
- Match the **colors and fonts** of your store branding
