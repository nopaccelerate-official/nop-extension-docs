The **Product Ribbon** allows store owners to add ribbons with text and images to various products in their nopCommerce store, including on the **Product Box** and **Product Detail** page.

This plug-in also supports **multi-store configuration**.

We provide **pre-defined message templates** that you can use as per your requirements.
