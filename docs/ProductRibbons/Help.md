Please drop us an email at **support@nopaccelerate.com** with issues you're facing. Alternatively, you can use support form at 
<http://www.nopaccelerate.com/wiki/support> to request support.

Please include the details below when you're emailing us:

1. Your Order Number:  
2. Store nopCommerce version:  
3. Plugin Name: Product Ribbons Plugin  
4. Plugin version:  
5. Store admin credentials (if possible):  
6. Reproduce steps:  
7. Is there any customization in your store:

It will be helpful if you attach images/screen-cast, so it will helpful in reproducing the issue at our end.

You can also email us steps for generating the issue at our end. For that you need to follow below steps.

- Open **“psr.exe”** from **Run** in your Windows operating system.  
- Click on **“Start Record”** button and perform the steps to reproduce your issue.  
- Click **“Stop Record”** button once you complete recording steps. It will ask you to save a zip file.  
- Send us saved zip file via email.
