1. **Reward Points Display on Product Pages:** Displays reward points on both the Product Details Page and Grouped Product Details Page, allowing customers to clearly see the points they will earn.
2. **Automatic Reward Point Calculation:** Automatically calculates reward points for products based on the store’s configured reward settings.
3. **Flexible Display Positioning:** Allows reward points to be displayed at a custom location on the product page using a configurable CSS selector.
4. **Customizable Content and Design:** Enables customization of reward message text, layout, and styling to match the store’s theme.
5. **Reward Points for Product Reviews:** Grants reward points when customers submit approved reviews for purchased products, awarded once per product per customer.
6. **Shared Reward Points:** Allows customers to transfer their earned reward points to other registered customers within the same store.
7. **Reward Points on Fast Purchase:** Provides bonus reward points when customers complete their purchase within a configured time after adding products to the cart.
8. **Reward Points on Specific Dates:** Awards additional reward points for orders placed on special promotional dates.
9. **Multi-Store Support:** Fully compatible with multi-store environments.
10. **Multi-Language Support:** Supports multiple languages From nopCommerce 4.10.
11. **Version Compatibility:** 3.80, 3.90, 4.00, 4.10, 4.20, 4.30, 4.40, 4.50, 4.60 , 4.70, 4.80 and 4.90
