# Installation & Licensing

Download the plug-in object code and place it in your **Plugins** folder, then follow the standard nopCommerce plug-in installation procedure.

The **Display Reward Points** plug-in appears under the **Promotions** group on the *Local plugins* page.

Once installed:

1. Go to your nopCommerce **Admin Panel**.
2. Navigate to:  
   **Configuration → Plugins → Local plugins → Display Reward Points plugin**
3. The plug-in will prompt you to enter your license key (as shown in the figure below).
4. Enter the license key you received via email after purchasing the plug-in from our website.
