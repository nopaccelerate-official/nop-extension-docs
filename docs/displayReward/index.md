# Display Reward Points Plugin

The **Display Reward Points Plugin** enhances the default nopCommerce reward system by clearly showing customers how many reward points they will earn when purchasing a product.

The reward points are displayed directly on the **Product Details Page**, helping customers understand the benefit before placing an order.

Customers can see the reward points information in a clearly styled section on the product page. The position, content, and design of this section can be configured to match the store theme and layout.

In addition to displaying reward points, the plugin introduces multiple ways for customers to earn and use reward points.

### 1. Reward Points for Product Review

After completing and receiving an order, customers can submit a review for the purchased product. Once the review is approved, reward points are added to their account.

- Points are granted only once per product per customer.

### 2. Shared Reward Points

Customers can transfer their earned reward points to another registered customer within the same store, allowing them to share benefits with friends or family.

### 3. Reward Points on Fast Purchase

When a product is added to the cart, a countdown timer begins. If the purchase is completed within the specified time, extra reward points are credited to the customer’s account.

### 4. Reward Points on Specific Date

On special promotional dates, customers who place orders on that day receive additional reward points automatically.
