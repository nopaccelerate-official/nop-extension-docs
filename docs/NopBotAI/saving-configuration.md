# Saving Your Configuration

After making any changes to the configuration, click the **Save** button located in the top-right corner of the configuration page. If the save is successful, you will see a confirmation message. Your changes will take effect immediately on the storefront.

## Quick Checklist Before Going Live

Before activating the chatbot on your store, confirm the following:

- **API Key** is entered and valid
- **Enable Plugin** and **Enable Chat Widget** are both checked
- **Primary Color** matches your store branding
- **Chat Header Title** and **Subtitle** are set to your preferred text
- **Live Preview** looks correct before saving
