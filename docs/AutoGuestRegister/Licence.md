Download plug-in object code and put it at your /Plug-in folder then follow nopCommerce standard plug-in installation procedure to install plug-in. Auto Guest Register Plug-in is available under **"Xcellence-IT”** group on local plug-ins page. Once Plug-in is installed go to your nopCommerce admin panel, then go to  

**nopAccelerate > Auto Guest > Configure**

It will ask you to enter your license key as shown in figure below, enter license key which you received on your registered email after purchasing this plug-in from our website.
